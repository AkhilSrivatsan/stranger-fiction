In life, we all have musical soulmates, artists whose sound is just so immediate to us. Artists whose most insane experiments flow through our bodies with barely any hindrance. Whose most inane compositions sound endearing. Miles Davis is one of my musical soulmates. I have yet to hear something by any iteration of Miles Davis that I haven’t enjoyed. Added to that list most recently is compilations from his fusion era — *Agharta*,*Pangaea*, and most recently,*Big Fun*. I recommend a languorous weekend spent with [*Big Fun*](https://open.spotify.com/album/66ubx5B6Q0Vgl7vbvGWP9h?si=nrsDlcDuSr6eMRYZzqk6Vw)* *filling the spaces in your bedroom as you contemplate whatever big questions occupy your life. It’ll be big fun.
