There are some songs and albums that go with certain memories like peanuts in plastic white cups used to go with Friday nights back in the day. In 2012, I had just graduated from college and had just begun life as a rent-paying adult. White men crooning soulfully over fuzzy, reverb-drenched, jangly guitars had become a large part of what indie rock sounded like. My Friday nights were spent sharing peanuts in plastic cups with friends, listening to, among other songs, Merchandise’s *Become What You Are*. My Saturday mornings were spent gulping glasses of water alone in my room listening to, among other albums, Merchandise’s *Children Of Desire*. Nostalgia is a wonderful thing.
