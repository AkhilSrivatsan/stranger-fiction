The basics of rock kind of remain the same. It's 🎸 + 🎸 + 🥁+🎤 = 🎶. That simple. But there's so much that can happen with those raw materials. Perhaps it's because my rabid love of music coincided with my discovery of post-punk, but I'm always blown away by simple evocative music. Drawing a bath and settling in for my nth listen-through of Canadian indie gold: Helena Deland’s [*Someone New*](https://open.spotify.com/album/6gwAAOZeKLYDAPzSSP4SCa?si=z5fGFuV8SsGA8Jr5_h7--g)*.*
