I’ve been unable to indulge in hip-hop much anymore. A weird thing that has begun happening with me and hip-hop is something happens whenever you hang out with your alco-weird friend. You’re always on the look out for a fight picked with a stranger, or inappropriate views about women or some other such in a litany of embarrassments. Anyway, I don’t feel that way about Prabh Deep’s [*K I N G*](https://open.spotify.com/album/5s22F5upYDZQiPVeYQZ7p7?si=7aZQ7TDCSdmx-ut4fgQE4w). In fact it’s one of my favourite records from 2019. It’s also likely my most listened to album from that year.
