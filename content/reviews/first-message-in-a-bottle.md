#### Today’s happy dispatch starts with a playlist.
[Join me at the top of this mountain](https://www.youtube.com/watch?list=PLMWENhvUL7VHvCkWZ09J5FCTpje0HjZ65&v=g0az4OkM02Y) is a playlist of alternative rock songs from the 80’s, 90’s, and 00’s. 

Its mood: not strictly happy-happy, but more like pump-your-fist-in-the-air, imagine-yourself-on-stage-singing, hate-your-ex kind of music. But it ends with *Don’t Stop Believin’* by *Journey*.
