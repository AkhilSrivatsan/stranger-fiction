Every one of Neil Cicierega’s [mashup](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mouth_Sounds) [albums](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mouth_Silence) [is](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mouth_Moods) [delightful](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mouth_Dreams). And the recently released [*Mouth Dreams*](http://www.neilcic.com/mouthdreams/) continues this trend. Cicierega’s music is comical, dripping with references to (American) pop culture, and always surprising. In Times Like These (TM), it feels good to have a few laughs and listen to happy-happy music.
