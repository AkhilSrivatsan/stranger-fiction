Some time in 2014, my friend and I were partaking in that quintessentially Bombay concept of a sharing table at that quintessentially Bombay institution, Janta, when we were joined by the lead singer of what was then, and is now, my favourite Indian band, with someone we assumed was a girlfriend, and someone we assumed was a girl friend. Bombay doesn't allow known faces private conversations.

Listen to [*Jaago*](https://open.spotify.com/album/6tldEdeKb9lNx13WArpPRJ?si=IGr575rRTiibLaAFh7AVrg) by Lifafa.
